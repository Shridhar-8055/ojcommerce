Priority Action Guide

Follow this order for maximum results. Start with quick wins and free platforms, then work your way up to high-authority publications.

Recommended Outreach Order

1

Sign Up for Featured.com + Source of Sources (Week 1)

Both are free. Start responding to journalist queries immediately. One placement can earn a DR 90+ backlink from Fortune, Yahoo, or Fast Company. This is the highest ROI activity.

2

Submit to Tier 2 Open Guest Post Sites (Week 1-2)

Home & Design Magazine and Smithers of Stamford have open "Write for Us" pages. Write and submit 2-3 articles. These are the easiest wins in the DR 60+ range.

3

Pitch Tier 2 Contributor Programs (Week 2-4)

Apply as a freelance contributor to Homedit, Hunker, Design Milk, and MyDomaine. These sites have active contributor programs and publish frequently.

4

Create BowerBird Profile (Week 2-3)

BowerBird is purpose-built for the home/design industry. It connects directly to Architectural Digest, Elle Decor, and House Beautiful. Essential for a furniture brand.

5

Pitch Tier 1 Publications (Month 2+)

Once you have 3-5 published articles from Tier 2 sites as writing samples, start pitching Apartment Therapy, Dwell, Houzz, and The Spruce. Use your published pieces as proof of quality.

6

Target Major Publications (Month 3+)

With a portfolio of published articles, pitch Better Homes & Gardens, Architectural Digest, Real Simple, and Forbes Home. These are long-term targets — one link from these sites is worth months of effort.
